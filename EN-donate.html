Foster Family

If you have the possibility, you can be a temporary foster family for one of our kittens, helping us provide better care.

Some kittens need special attention - orphaned babies, cats in recovery, or those who need socialization before final adoption.

Monthly Financial Support

If you wish, you can always sponsor a little friend and help with their food and healthcare expenses.

By sponsoring, you create a special connection with a specific kitten and your monthly support helps cover food, veterinary visits and treatments until they find a forever family.

About Us

Amigatos da Milkinha is a non-profit organization dedicated to saving the lives of our feline friends in honor of the indomitable spirit of our beloved Milka. With her captivating charm and boundless affection, she touched the heart of everyone she met and left a void that we promise to fill with purpose and compassion.

Our association is motivated by the belief that all furry friends deserve the opportunity for a happy and healthy life. We channel the love and inspiration we drew from Milka into a tireless effort to rescue, rehabilitate and rehome cats in need. Through our commitment to their wellbeing, we strive to create a world where every feline is cherished and receives the love and care they deserve.
